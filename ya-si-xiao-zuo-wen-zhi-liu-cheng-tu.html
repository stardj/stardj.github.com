雅思小作文之流程图

流程图

流程图题的审题步骤一般为：

  1. 时态（一般现在时）
  2. 语态（人类加工为被动语态，生命循环为主动语态）
  3. 提炼每一步的动词（注意词性的转换）
  4. 合理的时间顺序描述（顺序句式）
  5. 综述（一共有几步，从哪里开始到哪里结束）

How to describe different stages?顺序描述

矮穷矬版：连接词 高大上版：复杂句
Firstly After
Secondly Before
Finally So that
Next Until
Afterwards When
Then Where/Which
Following this/that This/These

状语从句

  1. 时间状语从句：

  2. At the first stage, they are cut, dried, and cooked.(连续动词简化), before/followed by being(注意语态) put in a machine.

  3. After that, the cooked apples are processed in a factory.

  4. 结果状语从句：

  5. Next, the processed apples are washed so that they are checked for quality.

  6. 条件状语从句：

  7. The life cycle begins when the adult moth lay eggs.

  8. The evaporating stage lasts for six hours until only coffee granules are left.

定语从句

  1. 地点为先行词的非限定性定语从句，补充解释“这里发生了什么”
  2. The are transported to a factory, where they are cut into pieces.
  3. Which引导的非限定性定语从句，补充说明
  4. The eggs grows to a larva, which is two centimeters long.

代词

The apples are cut into pieces, and these cut apples are cooked in a machine.

With短语

The coffee beans are cooked, with the oil being added in the vacuum.
